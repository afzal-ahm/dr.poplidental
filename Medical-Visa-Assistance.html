International Patient Services & Medical Visa Assistance

Welcome to Dr. Popli's Dental and Medical Care Centre. We take pride in being a premier destination for Traveling for Treatment, offering world-class clinical expertise combined with compassionate care. We understand that traveling abroad for healthcare requires meticulous planning, which is why we offer dedicated support For Our International Guests.

Visa Invitation Request & FRRO Support

To facilitate your journey, our clinic provides the necessary documentation to support your Medical Visa (Med-V) application.

  • Visa Invitation Request: Once your initial virtual consultation is complete and a treatment plan is proposed, we issue a formal Medical Invitation Letter.
  • FRRO Documentation & Visa Support: We assist in the mandatory Foreign National Registration process through the FRRO (Foreigners Regional Registration Office) portal to ensure your stay in Delhi is legally compliant and stress-free.
  • Extension Support: If your dental procedure requires a longer recovery time, we provide the clinical certificates necessary for visa extensions.

How to Start Your Journey

1
Virtual Consultation: Share your latest dental X-rays (OPG) or scans via email for a preliminary evaluation.
2
Treatment Plan & Quote: Receive a detailed breakdown of the procedure, timeline, and estimated costs.
3

FAQs for International Patients

Q: Can I apply for a tourist visa for dental treatment?

A: For extensive procedures, a Medical Visa is recommended as it allows for official FRRO registration and easier extensions if treatment plans change.

Q: How long does it take to receive the Invitation Letter?

A: After we receive your valid passport details and confirm the treatment plan, we typically issue the letter within 24–48 hours.

Q: Does the clinic help with local accommodation?

A: Yes, we can recommend reputable hotels and guest houses near our Yojana Vihar clinic to suit your budget.
